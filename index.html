Will She Say Yes?

A Diamond Is (Correction: Diamonds Are) Forever

You will be given a random engagement ring budget at the start of the game.

There are a few dazzling sparklers below. By clicking on a ring, you will be adding the ring to your shopping cart and adding thousands of dollars to the price tag.

She says "Yes!"" if you match your total price tag to the randomly generated ring budget. She says "No!" if your total shopping cart price tag exceeds the ring budget (tsk tsk! Shame on you for not being more responsible with your money!)

Unfortunately for you, the value of each rock is a mystery until you click on it to experience the sticker shock.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the price of each diamond as well as the ring budget you have to work with. Good luck!
